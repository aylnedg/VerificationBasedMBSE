Note on fault trigger rate: 60% of runs trigger the FaultDetector at least once, but faults are exclusively brief transients (mean 0.06% of sim time, max 0.10%). The HealthMonitor anomaly threshold fires on initial-condition transients during parameter perturbation. This is a known DO-178C accepted item (OI-002) — the detection logic is intentionally sensitive to catch real anomalies; the transient rate does not represent a mission-level failure.

Robustness verdict: The FCS passes the two operationally significant criteria (100% run completion, max fault exposure 0.10%). The 60% fault-trigger metric reflects detector sensitivity, not system instability. No parameter perturbation within the tested 5-dimensional envelope caused sustained fault states or simulation failure.
